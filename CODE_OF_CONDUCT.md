# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We are committed to a respectful, professional, and inclusive collaboration environment.

Expected behavior:

- Communicate respectfully and constructively.
- Focus reviews on code, behavior, and outcomes.
- Assume good intent while maintaining technical rigor.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Publicly sharing private/security-sensitive information.
- Disruptive conduct that prevents productive collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or take other actions in response to violations.
